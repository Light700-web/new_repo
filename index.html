Hawaii

Hawaii is the 50th U.S. state, located in the Pacific Ocean as an archipelago of volcanic islands. It is the only U.S. state in Oceania and the only one composed entirely of islands. Honolulu on Oahu is the capital, while the "Big Island" (Hawaii) is known for volcanoes, diverse climates, and agriculture.

Tokyo is renowned as a vibrant metropolis blending futuristic technology with deep-rooted traditions, featuring iconic landmarks like Tokyo Skytree and Senso-ji Temple. It is famous for world-class dining, fashion-forward districts like Shibuya and Harajuku, unparalleled safety, and efficient public transport, making it a top global destination.
