The Ukulele Isn’t Originally Hawaiian It’s strongly associated with Hawaii, but the ukulele was actually introduced by Portuguese immigrants. --- ### 🌊 9. Hawaii Grows Bigger Every Year Because of ongoing volcanic activity, new land is still being formed—so the islands are literally expanding. --- ### 🚫 10. Aloha Means More Than Just “Hello” “Aloha” can mean: * Love * Peace * Compassion * Hello/Goodbye It’s more of a lifestyle than just a word.
